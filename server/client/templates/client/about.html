Embark on an Enchanting Adventure.

Welcome to SEKGOENG CULLINAN LUXURY COTTAGES , where history and modernity intertwine in the heart of South Africa. Nestled in Brandbach, Cullinan, just a scenic 30-kilometer jaunt from Pretoria, our lodge emerges from the roots of the Highveld's savanna grasslands, born in 1853 by the hands of Johannes Malan, an intrepid settler and voortrekker.

Here, verdant thickets play host to a charming array of wildlife - duikers, jackals, squirrels, impalas, and the occasional kudu sighting. Our sanctuary beckons those enamored with nature's embrace and outdoor adventure.

Step into The SEKGOENG CULLINAN LUXURY COTTAGES, where opulent accommodations await. From expansive four-bedroom family suites to intimate single bedroom abodes, each retreat features a private lounge and a kitchenette. Standard amenities like television and Wi-Fi await, accessible and on the house. Engaging in the farming activities is an immersive choice, a chance to connect with your roots and partake in the land's ancient rhythms, just as our forebears did.
